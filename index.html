Recent Projects

Event-driven exploration of airfare prices and routes.

Work from the Citadel East Coast Invitational Datathon. My teammates and I used a Latent Dirichlet Allocation Model and regression analysis to see how event variety in major US cities affects airfare prices. We ended up winning 1st place!

Removing bias in sentiment analysis systems.

Final project for CIS 530 (Computational Linguistics). The goal of this project was to build a linguistic/statistical model that could predict the relative intensity of anger in a given text with minimized gender bias.

Determining key factors in university admissions.

Paper for STAT 474 (Modern Regression). Given data describing applications to a hypothetical university, I used the Generalized Additive Model to determine which factors are most relevant in deciding admission.
